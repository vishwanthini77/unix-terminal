## What You'll Learn

This course covers **5 essential lessons**:

1. **Navigation & File System Basics** - Move around directories, understand paths
2. **File Operations & Viewing Content** - Create, read, copy, move, and delete files
3. **Text Processing & Searching** - Find information in logs and files
4. **System Information & Processes** - Monitor resources and manage running programs
5. **Permissions & Practical Workflows** - Understand access rights and tie it all together

**Time commitment**: ~30-45 minutes per lesson. Complete at your own pace.

## What Makes Unix Important?

Unix powers the internet. Most servers, cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure), and development environments run Unix or Linux (a Unix variant).

**When you understand Unix, you can**:
- Debug production issues by reading logs
- Understand deployment processes
- Communicate precisely with engineers
- Navigate server environments confidently
- Make better technical decisions

**You don't need to be an expert** - just knowing the basics dramatically improves your effectiveness when working with technical teams.
